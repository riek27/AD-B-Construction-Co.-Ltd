About Us

Discover the story behind our commitment to excellence in Turkish furniture craftsmanship

Crafting Excellence

Weltew Homes embodies the rich heritage of Turkish furniture craftsmanship, blending centuries-old techniques with contemporary design. Each piece tells a story of meticulous attention to detail, premium materials, and artistic expression.

Based in Juba, South Sudan, we bring world-class luxury furniture to discerning clients who appreciate the fusion of traditional artistry and modern elegance. Our collections transform living spaces into sanctuaries of comfort and style.

Our Mission

At Weltew Homes, our mission is to enrich living spaces with furniture that combines aesthetic beauty with functional excellence. We believe that your home should be a reflection of your personality and a sanctuary for your soul.

We source only the finest materials and work with master craftsmen who share our passion for perfection. Every piece in our collection is designed to stand the test of time, both in durability and style.
